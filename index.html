About Me

Myfdlkmv first experience with computers and technology which I remember till date was making shapes with the help of LOGO which is a high-level programming language. I vividly remembered that in my fourth grade, my teacher taught the basics of how to make different shapes with LOGO. He also made a demo for showing us how to make a square by applying some simple commands and asked students to make a triangle by themselves. Luckily, I was the first in the lab to accomplish that task and our computer lab teacher appreciated me for that. When I reached to my eighth grade, my teacher started teaching us HTML. Thats when my enthusiasm for learning computers and programming increased how small changes affect the web page. After completion of my high school, I have no confusion on choosing stream because my natural inclination was choosing the field which was acquainted with computer.

After my undergraduate degree when I was working as a software engineer, I came across the interview of jeff bezos where he stated that Google is like a mountain, you can climb it but you can't just remove it. He stated that the one who have enormous data will rule the world.

After seeing couple of interviews of CEOs of big tech companies on evolving technology AI, I decided to persue my Masters degree with majors as Artificial Intelligence and Database Systems. In a long haul, I envision myself as a Data Scientist.

I am highly motivated Engineer with professional work experience in Software Engineering domain. Working in the field of software engineering I believe that I am a strong problem solver and have a good communication skills which will help me to excel in IT field. I am dedicated to exceed the goals and expectations.
